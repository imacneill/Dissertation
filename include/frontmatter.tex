\begin{abstract}
A search for Z boson production with associated top-antitop pairs is performed with a final state which contains tri-leptons and b-tagged jets. The measurement is performed on the complete dataset of pp collisions at a center-of-mass energy of 8 TeV collected at the CMS detector, for a total of \lumi = 19.5~\fbinv. A cross section is calculated for this final state and compared to the standard model prediction. The cross section for \ttbar~Z production is measured to be $\sigma=194 _{-89} ^{+105}$ \ fb. The measured cross section to theoretical cross section ratio is $0.94 _{-0.43} ^{+0.51}$. The process is measured with a significance of 2.33.


\end{abstract}
